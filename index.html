JOEL MILLER, is one of the central characters in The Last of Us,

known for his complex and morally grey personality. A survivor of a devastating fungal outbreak, Joel is a hardened, resourceful,

and skilled fighter who has endured great personal loss, including the tragic death of his daughter, Sarah. This trauma shapes his cold and distant demeanor, making him wary of forming emotional bonds. However,

when he's tasked with protecting a young girl named Ellie, Joel's fatherly instincts gradually re-emerge. Over time, he develops a deep, protective bond with Ellie, ultimately seeing her as a surrogate daughter.

Joel's actions, especially his controversial decisions to keep Ellie safe at all costs,
highlight his struggle between self-preservation and love, making him one of the most compelling and conflicted characters in modern video games.


ELLIEis a key character in The Last of Us,

known for her resilience, wit, and fierce determination. Born after the outbreak, Ellie has never known the world as it once was, but she possesses an inner strength that belies her young age.

Despite the horrors she has witnessed, Ellie retains a sense of hope and curiosity, often displaying a sharp sense of humor and a love for comic books and music.

What makes her truly remarkable is her immunity to the fungal infection that has decimated humanity, making her a symbol of hope for a possible cure. Throughout her journey with Joel, Ellie forms a deep emotional bond with him, viewing him as a father figure. However, her experiences also harden her, as she faces unimaginable losses and moral dilemmas.
